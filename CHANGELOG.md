# Changelog

All notable changes to the IoTFlow Android app. Format follows
[Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com).

## [1.1] — 2026-07-02 · versionCode 2

### Added
- Dashboard widgets: gauges, numbers, LED, status, plus switch/slider/button/terminal controls to trigger device actions
- Live line and bar charts drawn from telemetry history
- Automations tab: view your n8n flows, trigger them, and enable or disable them
- Alert notifications while the app is open
- Delete Account option in Settings

### Play submission
- Closed testing track, All Testers list, resubmitted 2026-07-02 (v1.0 previously submitted).

## [1.0] — 2026 · versionCode 1

### Added
- Initial release: sign in / register / demo mode, dashboard summary, devices (add, delete, detail, token), settings with configurable server.

### Play submission
- Initial closed-testing submission.
